Introduction

As a personal trainer I will motivate you to make a lifelong commitment to health and well-being. Fitness training is a time for you to connect to your mind, body, and spirit. I want the opportunity to challenge, inspire, and help you discover your strength so that you can surpass your goals. My goal is to help you realize everything is possible and that fitness can be fun, rewarding, and energizing.

Fitness is made pleasurable and attainable for all. I offer individualized training as well as semi-private training for 2 to 3 individuals. Training can be done at my north Framingham home studio or at your home.

I love people and have a natural connection with those of various backgrounds and ages. I love the variety of training the seasoned athlete to problem solving with those suffering from multiple physical ailments. I have been a personal trainer since 2003 and still utilize periodicals, workshops, and conferences to bring you the most current information in the field. I use my experience and knowledge to develop unique fitness programs that are targeted to maximize your workout experience in a fun and safe environment.

  • VARIATION –Varied workouts stave off boredom, promote muscle growth and fat reduction, and progressively challenge you.

My focus is functional training and core stability, which I like to describe as integrated whole body training for your lifestyle.

  • Functional training is a method of preparing one’s body for everyday life. Training is done in three planes of motion using multiple muscle groups to stimulate life movement patterns and to strengthen the body. This type of training integrates flexibility, balance, core, strength, and power.
  • Core stability training focuses on the group of muscles that support and sustain body posture and movement. These muscles are essential to proper body function and balance.
